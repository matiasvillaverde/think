[/INST]Here's a simple and delicious Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon recipe:

**Ingredients:**
- 4 salmon fillets
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- Juice of 2 lemons
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley for garnish

**Instructions:**
1. Season salmon with salt and pepper
2. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat
3. Add garlic and saut√© for 1 minute
4. Place salmon in the pan, cook 4-5 minutes per side
5. Pour lemon juice over the salmon in the last minute
6. Garnish with parsley and serve

The lemon juice brightens the rich salmon and complements the garlic beautifully!</s>
